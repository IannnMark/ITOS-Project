Mobile and Games Platforms


Mobile Platform means any network or service that uses mobile communications standards and protocols for the delivery of voice, data and audio-visual services


What are the 2 mobile platforms?

  • Google's Android
  • Apple's iOS

Android

Android is a mobile operating system based on a modified version of the Linux kernel and other open-source software, designed primarily for touchscreen mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets.


Apple

iOS is a mobile operating system developed by Apple Inc. exclusively for its hardware. It is the operating system that powers many of the company's mobile devices
