Security


In today's world, gadgets have become an integral part of our lives. From smartphones to laptops, tablets to wearables, gadgets have revolutionized the way we live, work, and communicate. However, with the increasing use of gadgets, security has become a major concern. Cyber threats, data breaches, and hacking attacks have become common, posing a threat to the privacy and security of our personal information.


Different Platform Technologies Applied for Security


  • Secure Boot
  • Encryption
  • Two-Factor Authentication

Secure Boot


Secure boot is a platform technology that ensures that only trusted software is loaded on a device during startup. This technology checks the digital signature of the software and ensures that it has not been tampered with or modified. This helps to prevent malware and other malicious software from being loaded onto the device during startup.


Encryption


Encryption is a platform technology that is used to protect data stored on a device. Encryption works by converting data into an unreadable format that can only be deciphered using a specific key. This helps to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive data, even if the device is lost or stolen.


Two Factor Authentication


Two-factor authentication (2FA) is a security technology that requires users to provide two forms of identification before accessing a device or application.
